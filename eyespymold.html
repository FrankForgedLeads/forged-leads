Mold Inspection, Testing &
Remediation Services

We handle every step — from finding it to fixing it

Mold Inspection

We walk your entire property looking for mold, moisture, and the conditions that cause mold to grow. Using moisture meters and thermal cameras we find what's hiding inside walls and ceilings — plus we always check your AC system, one of the most overlooked spots in Florida homes. You receive a written report with photos documenting everything we found.

Mold Testing & Lab Analysis

We take air samples and surface swabs from problem areas and send them to a certified independent lab. The lab identifies what type of mold is there and how concentrated the spores are in your air — compared to outdoor levels so you know if your home is actually affected. Results usually return within 24–72 hours.

Mold Remediation (Cleanup)

We seal off the contaminated area so spores can't spread, run industrial HEPA air scrubbers to pull spores from the air, remove all affected materials, apply antimicrobial treatment, and retest after to confirm the mold is completely gone. Every job is documented and done to IICRC industry standards.

Specialty Services

Indoor Air Quality Testing

We test what you're actually breathing. Important for families with asthma, allergies, young children, or anyone feeling consistently unwell at home. We measure airborne mold spore levels and compare to outdoor baselines.

Water Damage Mold Inspection

Mold begins growing within 24–48 hours after any water intrusion. We use thermal imaging and moisture meters to find it inside walls and under floors before it spreads further and costs more to address.

Insurance Claim Documentation

Insurance companies require specific documentation to approve mold claims. We write our reports the way adjusters need them — detailed photos, lab results, moisture readings, and scope of damage — so your claim moves faster.

Commercial Mold Inspection

Mold in a business means liability exposure, employee health concerns, and potential downtime. We inspect offices, retail spaces, warehouses, and multi-unit buildings and provide documentation that meets commercial standards.

Real Estate Mold Inspection

Buying or selling? A mold inspection protects buyers from post-closing surprises and gives sellers documented proof the property is clean. Our reports are trusted by Florida real estate agents and help deals close without mold disputes.

Built for Florida's Climate

Florida conditions create the perfect environment for mold — we know exactly where it hides.

Year-Round Humidity

Florida indoor humidity stays above 50% almost every day — and 50% is exactly the level where mold starts to grow. We know every spot it likes to hide in Florida homes.

Hurricane & Storm Damage

Post-storm water getting into your walls and ceilings is the #1 cause of mold in Florida. Mold can start growing within 24 hours of a flood or leak — we respond fast after any storm event.

AC System Mold

Your AC runs almost every day in Florida. The drain pan, coils, and ductwork collect moisture and can grow mold silently, spreading spores through your vents into every room. We always check the AC.

Condo & High-Rise Problems

Shared walls, building envelope failures, and moisture in concrete create mold problems in Florida condos that require specific experience to properly diagnose. We've inspected hundreds.

Hidden Water Intrusion

Roof leaks, plumbing failures, and moisture rising through concrete slabs are common in Florida — and they hide inside walls for months before becoming visible. We find them with thermal imaging.

South Florida Salt & Heat

From Miami's salt air to Broward's extreme humidity to Palm Beach storm exposure — coastal Florida is especially aggressive for mold growth. We tailor every inspection to your specific area and building type.
