Our Lead Qualification Process

How We Verify Every Lead Before You Get It

1

Data Sourcing

Our system pulls from permit filings, insurance claims, property damage databases, and direct homeowner inquiries across Florida. No bought lists. No recycled data.

2

AI Pre-Screening

Our AI analyzes each lead for project type, budget range, property ownership, geographic match, and timeline urgency. Leads that don't fit get filtered out automatically.

3

Live Phone Verification

A real team member calls the homeowner and confirms project details, timeline, and budget range. No robo-calls. No form-fill-only leads.

4

Qualification Scoring

Each lead is scored 1–100 based on readiness, budget, and urgency. Leads scoring below 60 go back into our nurture sequence — not dumped on you.

5

Exclusive Delivery

The qualified lead is sent to one contractor — you — with the homeowner's name, number, project details, and preferred appointment time. Show up and close.

I sat in the trucks, climbed onto the roofs, and watched up close how jobs get won — and how good money gets wasted on junk leads.

I'm not a marketer who discovered contractors. I spent a decade in your world — and built the lead system I wish you'd had the whole time.

I watched contractors pay Angi, HomeAdvisor, and “lead companies” for the same lead they sold to four other guys — then spend their nights chasing renters, tire-kickers, and dead phone numbers. That's not a lead problem. That's a system problem.

Hive is the fix. We don't sell websites, and we don't sell you software to figure out yourself. We do the sourcing, the calling, and the screening for you — and hand you exclusive Florida homeowners who are ready for a quote. You show up, you estimate, you close.

Everything we do is measured against one question: does this put another booked job on your calendar? If it doesn't, we don't do it.
