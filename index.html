Our Lead Qualification Process

How We Verify Every Lead Before You Get It

1

Data Sourcing

Our system pulls from permit filings, insurance claims, property damage databases, and direct homeowner inquiries across Florida. No bought lists. No recycled data.

2

AI Pre-Screening

Our AI analyzes each lead for project type, budget range, property ownership, geographic match, and timeline urgency. Leads that don't fit get filtered out automatically.

3

Live Phone Verification

A real team member calls the homeowner and confirms project details, timeline, and budget range. No robo-calls. No form-fill-only leads.

4

Qualification Scoring

Each lead is scored 1–100 based on readiness, budget, and urgency. Leads scoring below 60 go back into our nurture sequence — not dumped on you.

5

Exclusive Delivery

The qualified lead is sent to one contractor — you — with the homeowner's name, number, project details, and preferred appointment time. Show up and close.
